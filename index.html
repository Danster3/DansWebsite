About

Proudly owned and operated on the Kapiti Coast by Daniel and Sarah Watson. Daniel grew up in a small community in Southland with around 60 people in the township. Daniel started training as an Electrician back in 2012, married Sarah in 2017 then moved up to her home town in Kapiti. Daniel has a range of experience across all areas of electrical but is vastly experienced and passionate about household electrical work.

Daniel started Mr Watts Electrical in 2019 because he wanted to have the platform to give back into the community and have the freedom to build relationships with his clients. Our goal is not to grow a massive company, but to maintain personal relationships with all of our clients.

We offer 24/7 callouts and work Monday to Saturday with no extra fees, we value mental health and electrical safety, so if you're worried and have questions about anything electrical in your house please feel free to call us for advice on 0278414557.
